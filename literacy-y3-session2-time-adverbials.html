LEARN — TIME WORDS

Discovering words that answer "When?"

⏱ 5–20 min

🧠 What's an Adverbial?

An adverbial is a word or phrase that tells us more about the verb (the action). A time adverbial tells us WHEN the action happened!

⏰ TIME WORD COLLECTION — Click to Collect!

Click each word to add it to your collection! 🌟

🌱 WATCH A SENTENCE GROW!

I played outside.

👇 Click each version to see how the time word fits in:

END I played outside today.
END I played outside in the afternoon.
START Yesterday, I played outside.

✨ Time words can go at the START or the END of a sentence!
